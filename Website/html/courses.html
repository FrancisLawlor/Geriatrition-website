Courses

In addition to standalone classes, we offer fitness courses to our members.

Courses consist of a collection of classes which occur sequentially over a period of six weeks. Courses are available to members only, though we do offer one free session to help you in deciding if our community is for you.

Although it is recommended that participants attend all sessions as part of our courses in order to get as much as possible out of the experience, courses are designed to ensure that those who miss sessions will not be left behind.

Our courses include:

Course Description
Yoga A combination of physical postures, breathing exercises and methods of meditation that have been practiced for thousands of years are utilised in order to help you to strengthen muscles, increase flexibility and lower blood pressure.
Pilates A vast collection of unique exercises which will improve strength, posture, balance and flexibility. Similar to yoga in a lot of ways, though emphasis is placed on exercising the core.
Zumba A Latin-inspired dance fitness-party that’s easy to follow, effective and tremendously enjoyable. It’s a course which is growing in popularity and which will likely become the highlight of your week!
