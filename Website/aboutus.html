About Us

Welcome to GERIATRITION!

GERIATRITION Ltd. was formed in 2011 by a group of professionals in medical, health and leisure industries. Collectively they identified that the traditional gym environment can be somewhat alienating for people in their later years and that this keeps a lot of people over the age of 65 from getting to enjoy physical exercise in a monitored and controlled space.

GERIATRITION provides services in more than twenty gyms and leisure centres around the country. Our priority is ensuring that YOU, the client is satisfied by your time with us and enjoys an opportunity to reclaim your vitality and make new friends.

We will be here to guide you with structured programs and direction where you need it, so that you can achieve the results you desire.

Accessibility

An important aspect of what we do at GERIATRITION is ensuring that the members of our community are able to utilise our services easily and comfortably. For that reason, when deciding whether or not to provide our services in any given establishment we make sure that all buildings and carparks are in compliance with all requirements associated with universal access.
